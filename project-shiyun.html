Overview

Shiyun (椋熻繍) is a cookware system designed to support a seamless transition from cooking to eating. Featuring a dual-function lid that serves as both a utensil rest and a trivet, the design transforms everyday stove-to-table convenience into a deliberate ritual.

By combining cast iron performance with thoughtful interaction, Shiyun reduces cleanup while creating a more grounded and intentional dining experience.
